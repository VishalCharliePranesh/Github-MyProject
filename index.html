Pro Kitchen Tips

Cook Like a Chef

Simple principles that elevate every meal from good to unforgettable.

🧂

Season in Layers

Add salt at each stage of cooking, not just at the end. Building flavour in layers creates depth you can't achieve otherwise.

🌡️

Rest Your Meat

After cooking, let meat rest for at least 5–10 minutes. This redistributes juices and makes every bite far more succulent.

🔪

Sharp Knives Only

A sharp knife is your single most important kitchen tool. It's safer, faster, and produces cleaner cuts for better texture.
